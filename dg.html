here's a simple way to downgrade that is easily understood by skids step 1: go to chrome100.dev and find your chromebook board or model. then click show more. step 2: download the version you'd like to install step 3: open the chromebook recovery utility on a mac or pc (or chromebook if unblocked) step 4: in the top right, click settings, then use local image step 5: wait step 6: open the chromebook step 7: press esc + ref(↺) + power insert the usb you used wait step 8: you should now be on a lower version of chromeOS. congrats.
